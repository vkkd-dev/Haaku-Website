Patient

Care 24x7

Client

The Client

Patient Care 24x7 is a top HealthTech company that provides round-the-clock patient care services. They specialize in doctor and ambulance booking, making it easy for patients to access the medical attention they need quickly and efficiently. With their user-friendly platform and dedicated team of healthcare professionals, Patient Care 24x7 is committed to ensuring the best possible care for every patient. Choose Patient Care 24x7 for your healthcare needs and experience top-notch service that you can rely on.

Problem

The Problem

Patient Care 24x7 addresses the problem of timely access to medical care by offering a convenient and reliable platform for doctor and ambulance booking. They understand that quick medical attention is crucial in many situations, and their services help to ensure that patients receive the care they need when they need it. But without a website or app, it was difficult for patients to access the services they needed or for potential customers to learn more about the company and its offerings.

Solution

Solution

Our Solution

PatientCare24*7 decided to work with our developers to create a website and app that would serve as a central hub for information about the company and its services. The app would also allow patients to access care and communicate with healthcare professionals anytime, anywhere. In addition, the app includes a feature that allows patients to easily book an air ambulance in case of an emergency or for scheduled transport to and from medical appointments.

Working with our team of developers and designers, PatientCare24*7 defined the key features and functionlities they wanted to include in the app, such as secure messaging with healthcare professionals, the ability to schedule appointments, and access to medical records. They also partnered with local ambulance providers to ensure that patients have access to reliable and timely transportation.

The website and app were designed with a focus on creating a seamless and user-friendly experience for patients. Team Haaku implemented security measures to ensure patient data is protected, and conducted user testing to ensure that the products were easy to use and met the needs of their target audience.

Result

Outcome

The Outcome

The creation of a website and app for PatientCare24*7 represents a significant step forward for the company in terms of expanding its reach and improving patient care. With these digital tools in place, patients can access care and communicate with healthcare professionals at any time, from anywhere, making it easier for them to manage their health and well-being. The inclusion of ambulance booking services also provides a one-stop-shop for patients seeking round-the-clock care and transportation, making it easier for them to manage their healthcare needs. They have recently been selected as one of the finalist for Eureka! 2022 (IIT Bombay).

In conclusion, PatientCare24*7's website and app have helped the company reach more patients, provide better care, and streamline the process of booking ambulance services. These digital tools have positioned the company as a leader in the healthcare space, leveraging Ambulance Dispatch Software, Healthcare App Development, and Remote Patient Monitoring technologies to improve patient outcomes and revolutionize the healthcare industry.
