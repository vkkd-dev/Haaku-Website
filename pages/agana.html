Angana

Jewellery

Assamese Traditional Jewellery

Client

The Client

Angana Jewellery is a direct-to-consumer brand specializing in authentic Assamese traditional jewellery. The brand is committed to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Assamese jewellery-making, while also providing customers with high-quality, ethically-sourced jewellery at affordable prices. All of Angana Jewellery's pieces are handcrafted by skilled artisans using traditional techniques that have been passed down through generations. Each piece is a unique expression of Assamese culture and craftsmanship. The brand offers a wide variety of traditional Assamese jewellery styles, including necklaces, earrings, bracelets, and more. Customers can shop with confidence knowing that they are supporting an ethical and sustainable brand that values the artistry and traditions of Assamese jewellery-making.

Solution

Solution

Our Solution

At Haaku, we understand that color plays a crucial role in creating a strong and recognizable brand identity. When Angana Jewellery approached us for help with their brand's color palette, we were eager to provide solutions that would help them stand out in a crowded market.

Our team of experts worked closely with Angana Jewelry to understand their brand values and aesthetic preferences. Based on their input, we developed a custom color palette that would capture the essence of their brand and resonate with their target audience. The color palette we developed for Angana Jewellery is inspired by the vibrant and colorful culture of Assam. It features a range of warm, earthy tones that reflect the natural beauty of the region, as well as bold, bright colors that evoke the energy and passion of Assamese tradition. With our custom color palette, Angana Jewellery now has a unique and recognizable brand identity that sets them apart from their competitors. Their website and marketing materials feature a cohesive and harmonious color scheme that creates a memorable and visually appealing experience for their customers.
